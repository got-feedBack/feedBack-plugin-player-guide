Player Controls

Everything in the playback bar, explained.

▶

Playback

Start and stop the song, or jump by five seconds.

⏪ 5s Seek back five seconds
▶ Play Toggle play / pause
5s ⏩ Seek forward five seconds
🎸

Arrangement

Switch between the parts available for this song — Lead, Rhythm, Bass, and Vocals. Each has its own note chart and difficulty curve.

Not all songs include every part.

🐢

Playback Speed

Drag the slider to slow the song down — all the way to 25 % for the trickiest passages. The audio pitch is preserved so your ears don't suffer.

25 %
150 %
🎯

Difficulty

Filters out harder notes at lower values, showing you a simplified version of the chart. Great for beginners or when learning a new song one section at a time.

Simplified
Full chart
🎚️

Stem Mixer

Click Stem Mixer to open per-stem volume sliders. Available stems depend on the song — typically guitar, bass, drums, and vocals.

Tip: duck the guitar to 0 % and practice against the backing track.

🎤

Lyrics

Toggles a lyrics overlay on the highway. Only visible when the Lyrics Sync plugin has generated a lyric track for this song.

Button is greyed out when no lyrics are available.

🖥️

Visualization & Quality

Visualization swaps the renderer — Default (2D highway), Auto (matches the arrangement type), or any installed viz plugin such as 3D Highway.

Quality scales the canvas render resolution. Drop to Medium or Low if the highway feels sluggish.

🔁

A / B Loop

Mark a section to repeat endlessly:

A Set loop start at the current position
B Set loop end — playback now loops A → B
Save Name and store this loop for later
✕ Clear Remove the current loop markers
